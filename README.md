# Domestic Staffing Hub

Domestic Staffing Hub is a polished service website for connecting households, families, hotels, restaurants, offices, and businesses with reliable domestic support. The website presents the brand as professional, trustworthy, and easy to reach, while giving visitors clear paths to request staff, explore services, view gallery proof, and contact the team.

The site is designed around the brand promise:

> Quality People, Trusted Service, Peace of Mind.

## Website Purpose

The website helps Domestic Staffing Hub turn online visitors into real inquiries. It gives clients a clear understanding of the services available, builds trust through structured content and visuals, and makes it easy to start a request through forms, WhatsApp, phone, email, and social channels.

It also separates job applicants from clients. People looking for work are not pushed into the main customer journey; they use a separate "I Need a Job" page that is accessible from the footer.

## What The Website Does

- Presents Domestic Staffing Hub as a trusted staffing service brand.
- Shows available domestic and business support services.
- Allows clients to request staff through a structured service request form.
- Opens WhatsApp conversations with a clean, pre-filled message for clients.
- Provides a separate applicant flow for people who want to work with Domestic Staffing Hub.
- Displays a public gallery for service photos and brand activity.
- Includes an Admin Center for managing gallery images without editing the website code.
- Makes contact options easy to find through phone, WhatsApp, email, Instagram, and Facebook.

## Main Services

The website highlights staffing support for:

- Chefs
- Drivers and chauffeurs
- Home tutors
- Caregivers
- Hospitality support
- Custom domestic service needs

Each service is presented in a way that helps clients understand what kind of support they can request and how Domestic Staffing Hub can help them find the right person.

## Client Journey

The site is structured so a client can quickly move from interest to action:

1. A visitor lands on the homepage and understands the brand immediately.
2. They explore services or go directly to the request form.
3. They submit their service need through the website form or start a WhatsApp chat.
4. Service request details are sent to the operations channel for follow-up.
5. The team can contact the client and continue the conversation.

The WhatsApp message for clients is written differently from the applicant message, so the team can quickly understand whether someone is requesting a service or looking for a job.

## Applicant Journey

The applicant side is intentionally kept separate from the main customer experience. The "I Need a Job" page allows people who can provide domestic services to submit a simple WhatsApp message with:

- Name
- Service they can provide

This keeps the applicant flow simple and allows the Domestic Staffing Hub team to continue the conversation directly on WhatsApp.

## Gallery

The gallery gives the website a more active and trustworthy feel by showing real service-related images and updates. Visitors can view gallery items from the public site, and each image can be opened for a closer look.

The Admin Center allows authorized users to:

- Add gallery images
- View uploaded gallery items
- Edit gallery titles and categories
- Delete outdated images
- Change the admin password

This makes it easier for the business owner or team to keep the website fresh without calling a developer every time they want to add a new image.

## Brand Experience

The redesign gives Domestic Staffing Hub a stronger and more modern online identity. The visual style is clean, confident, and service-focused, with a color direction that better matches the brand logo while still feeling fresh.

The website avoids unnecessary write-ups and keeps the important content direct. Instead of overexplaining, it guides visitors through clear pages, strong service sections, contact options, and simple actions.

## Key Pages

- **Home:** Introduces the brand, services, gallery preview, and main actions.
- **About:** Shares the company story, mission, and vision.
- **Services:** Displays the domestic and business support categories.
- **Service Details:** Gives each service its own focused page.
- **Contact:** Lets clients request staff and reach the team directly.
- **Gallery:** Shows uploaded service and brand images.
- **I Need a Job:** Separate applicant page for people who want to work with the brand.
- **Admin Center:** Private gallery management area for authorized users.
